# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

✅ Welcoming, inclusive, empathetic language  
❌ No harassment, insults, or unwelcome advances

## Enforcement

Report violations to conduct@expenseflow.com. All reports are reviewed fairly and confidentially.
